Doughnut Trivia

The first Friday in June is "National Donut Day" in the USA. The holiday was started by the Chicago Salvation Army in 1928 to celebrate Salvation Army lassies who volunteered their cooking skills in Europe. One of the foods that they made for the troops was donuts.
