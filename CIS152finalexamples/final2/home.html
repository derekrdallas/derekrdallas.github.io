Welcome to Doughnut Delights

Doughnut Delights has been serving CU with the highest quality doughnuts and coffees since 1971. Nestled in downtown Urbana, Doughnut Delights has become a haven for “Gotta have it” breakfast treats. Our doughnuts are baked fresh twice daily and our coffee’s brewed every fifteen minutes. If you have never been to CU’s hidden gem then you are missing out.

 

Freshly Roasted Coffee

Here at Doughnut Delights we ground and roast our coffee grounds everyday so that you can get that fresh coffee to wake you up. Whether you just get up, on your way to work, taking your youngster to school or what ever you are goinging to do throughout the day, make sure you wake up with our coffee. If coffee isn't for you. Don't worry, here at Doughnut Delights we have you covered we brew fresh just about any tea you could possibly want. If we don't have it we will find it for your next visit.

Yes, we care about our customers that much.

 

 

Freshly made doughnuts

That is right. We make our doughnuts fresh everyday just for you. Whether you are on your way to work, school, or just your everyday dealings. Our doughnuts are right for anything and everything you do.
