Welcome to Doughnut Delights!

The Finest in breakfast treats and coffees since 1971

Doughnut Delights has been serving C-U with the highest quality doughnuts and coffees since 1971. Nestled in downtown Urbana, Doughnut Delights has become a haven for “Gotta have it” breakfast treats. Our doughnuts are baked fresh twice daily and our coffee’s brewed every fifteen minutes.If you have never been to C-U’s hidden gem then you are missing out.

Did You Know: Doughnut Day is celebrated the first Friday of June each year.
